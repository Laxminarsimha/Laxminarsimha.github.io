Greetings from Osmania University and Welcome to Osmania University’s Web Site. I invite you to explore our site and see for yourself the many resources available that makes this University unique. Osmania University, established in 1918, is the seventh oldest in India, the third oldest in south India and the first to be established in the erstwhile princely state of Hyderabad. Through out its existence of over eight decades, it has shown remarkable progress and sustained an integrated development of all faculties. It has significantly contributed to the academic and economic development of not only the region but also of the Country.
